Welcome to
Dots & Boxes!

The aim of the game is to create the most amount of boxes
compared to your opponent.

Here's how to play:

  • - Take it in turns to click on a line to mark it with your colour

  • - Four lines are needed (regardless of colour) to make up a box.

  • - The person who clicks on the fourth line of the box
    will create it, and get another turn.

  • - The more boxes you create the more turns you get.

  • - The person with the most boxes created will be the winner.
